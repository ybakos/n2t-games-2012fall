Notes for elements project:

Functions:

playGame:
starts a new game of men's morris. Calls humanTurn to begin the game.  

drawBoard:
implement on Sunday

placePieces:
takes an input of the spot the piece should be placed on. Redraws the board with that piece on the spot choosen. 

computerTurn:
Calls placePieces with a randomly generated spot from the open spots that are left. 

humanTurn:
Calls placePieces with an input from the user. Validates the spot given as an open spot.

checkForWin:
checks the board for a winning condition. If one player has less than two pieces left they have lost and the other player has won. Also if one player cannot move, the other player wins.
